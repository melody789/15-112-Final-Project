Hangzhou is the capital of Zhejiang Province and the local 
political, economic and cultural center. As the southern 
terminus of the Grand Canal, the city is located on the 
lower reaches of the Qiantang River in southeast China, a 
superior position in the Yangtze Delta and only 112 mi 
(180 km) from Shanghai.The subtropical monsoon climate 
contributes to varied seasonal sceneries, making Hangzhou 
one of China's most popular travel destinations all the 
year round.
