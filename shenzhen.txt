Shenzhen is located in the southern portion of the Guangdong 
Province, on the eastern shore of the Pearl River Delta. 
Neighboring the Pearl River Delta and Hong Kong, Shenzhen's 
location gives it a geographical advantage for economic 
development. In 1980, the first Special Economic Zone of 
China was built in this city. From then on, the city become 
a highlight of China, one known for its rapid economic growth.
