Yunnan, which refers to 'the place south of the colorful clouds',
or 'the place south of Yunling (cloudy ridge) Mountain', is the
most southwest province of China. Externally, it borders Vietnam,
Laos and Burma; internally, it is neighbor to Guizhou, Guangxi,
Chongqing, Sichuan and Tibet. It is a land of various ethnic 
groups, landscapes, natural scenery, creatures, etc where one 
could taste the most aspects of our planet.
